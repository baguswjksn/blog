Last week, I read a book on personal finance called Kakeibo — the traditional Japanese method of mindful budgeting. It was a simple, elegant system, designed to make you think before spending, reflect regularly, and grow a stronger relationship with your money.
But there was one problem: it was all on paper.
Kakeibo encourages you to carry a notebook, record your expenses manually, reflect weekly and monthly, and write down your financial goals. Beautiful in theory — but impractical when you're always on the go.
I thought: What if I could build a Telegram bot that helps me follow the Kakeibo method digitally?
I already use Telegram daily. It's fast, always with me, and bots are surprisingly easy to build. Plus, I wanted a tool that didn't overwhelm me with charts and features — just something that asked the right questions, kept things simple, and stayed out of my way.
